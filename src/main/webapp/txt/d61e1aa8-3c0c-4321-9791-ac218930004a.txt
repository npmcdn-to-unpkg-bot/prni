  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This record holds a gridded bathymetry dataset of the Port Havannah area, on the northern part of Efate Island in Vanuatu collected in 1990. This survey was conducted as part of the work carried out from 25 August to 10th September 1992 as part of the 1992 SOPAC Work Plan Task 92.Va.14a: Study of Forarl Bay manganese. A Raytheon De719e echo sounder was used for profiling the seabed. Tidal correction information was obtained from Admiralty tide tables for Vila and data reduced to LAT (Lowest Astronomical Tide).
