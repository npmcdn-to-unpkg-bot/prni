  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This record holds the Navigation Map, Sheet F15 of the North New Hebrides Back Arc Area entirely located in Solomon Islands EEZ. The map is part of the SOPACMAPS [Project Sheets 1-25] collection produced for the SOPACMAPS Project Leg 2 cruise that was conducted by the oceanographical research vessel L'Atalante. Prior to the at-sea operations, a sheet assemblage (at 1/250,000 scale) was established and used during the survey and for the processing of the first set of charts. The swath mapping project was funded by the EEC (Lome III - European Development Fund No 6) known as SOPACMAPS, and was awarded to IFREMER. A copy of the map can be downloaded by selecting the appropriate link under 'Online resources' from this record with permission from the Secretariat.

  │   └─Supplemental information…………………………………………… SOPACMAPS Leg 2, Cruise ID: AL9302, Project: Swath mapping areas of seafloor, Chiefs Scientist: Jean-Marie Auzende, Institutions: EEC, ORSTOM, IFREMER. The SOPACMAPS Project was designed to investigate as much area as possible of the EEZs of the SOPAC member countries: Solomon Islands, Vanuatu, Fiji and Tuvalu.The main objectives were to map the bathymetry, investigate the resource potential and to study geological processes, including geological hazards. The 85 m R/V L'Atalante, launched in 1990, was built specifically for swath mapping surveys and carries a dual Simrad EM12 multibeam bathymetric system. The schedule of the cruises was: * Leg 1- 19 July in Noumea (New Caledonia) to 15 August 1993 in Honiara (Solomon Islands), * Leg 2 - 19 August in Honiara (Solomon Islands) to 16 September 1993 in Suva (Fiji), * Leg 3 - 22 September in Suva (Fiji) to 20 October 1993 in Noumea (New Caledonia).
