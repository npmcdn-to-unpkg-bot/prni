  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… The Pacific Islands Applied Geoscience Commission, SOPAC, carried out a marine survey for Tonga around the islands of Tongatapu and Niuafo'ou and Tongatapu and Fangauta lagoon. The map shows the single beam bathymetry of Tongatapu in November/December 2003. This metadata entry includes two derived bathymetry files and a smoothed bathymetry file of Tongatapu. ¶ These were referenced to chart datum. Datasets include a MIKE bathymetry file, using flexible mesh which can be used to generate 3D picture for presentation. Also included is a resulting 3D picture showing data sources used to create the bathymetry. ¶ This work was initiated by the SOPAC/EU Reducing Vulnerability of Pacific ACP States Project.
