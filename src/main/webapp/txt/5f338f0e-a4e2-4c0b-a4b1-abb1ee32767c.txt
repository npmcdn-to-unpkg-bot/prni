  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This record holds the Gravity Map, Sheet 16 of Pandora Bank Area (PBA) which is located at the most eastern termination of Solomon Islands EEZ. The map is part of the SOPACMAPS [Project Sheets 1-25] collection, produced for the SOPACMAPS Project Leg 2 cruise that was conducted at the transit from Pandora Bank Area to Suva by the oceanographical research vessel L'Atalante in September 14, 1993. Prior to the at-sea operations, a sheet assemblage (at 1/250,000 scale) was established and used during the survey and for the processing of the first set of charts. Gravimetre/Anomalie/air libre, Annotation en mgals, Pas d'échantillonnage: 90 secondes (written in French on the map). The swath mapping project was funded by the EEC (Lome III - European Development Fund No 6) known as SOPACMAPS, and was awarded to IFREMER. A copy of the map can be downloaded by selecting the appropriate link under 'Online resources' from this record with permission from the Secretariat.

  │   └─Supplemental information…………………………………………… The SOPACMAPS Project was designed to investigate as much area as possible of the EEZs of the SOPAC member countries: Solomon Islands, Vanuatu, Fiji and Tuvalu.The main objectives were to map the bathymetry, investigate the resource potential and to study geological processes, including geological hazards. The 85 m R/V L'Atalante, launched in 1990, was built specifically for swath mapping surveys and carries a dual Simrad EM12 multibeam bathymetric system. The schedule of the cruises was: * Leg 1- 19 July in Noumea (New Caledonia) to 15 August 1993 in Honiara (Solomon Islands), * Leg 2 - 19 August in Honiara (Solomon Islands) to 16 September 1993 in Suva (Fiji), * Leg 3 - 22 September in Suva (Fiji) to 20 October 1993 in Noumea (New Caledonia).
