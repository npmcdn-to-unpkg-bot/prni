  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… A single beam bathymetry survey was carried out in Tongatapu's Fanga'uta lagoon. Where an Echotrac paper plotter was used to collect single beam bathymetry data for the whole lagoon. A small boat carrying the equipment was used, where it followed drawn lines in Hypack max software at 200m intervals, each line was numbered and data was recorded according to the line surveyed. This work was initiated by the SOPAC/EU Reducing Vulnerability of Pacific ACP States Project
  │   ├─Purpose………………………………………………………………………………………… The purpose of the survey was to understand the marine movement in the lagoon, which can help understand the dynamics that occur within the lagoon, from coastal erosion and flushing rates.
