  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This record holds a Morpho-Structural Map of the East Malaita in the Solomon Islands. The map is part of the SOPACMAPS, Leg 2 collection produced for the SOPACMAPS Project cruise conducted by the oceanographical research vessel L'Atalante in 1993. The swath mapping project was funded by the EEC (Lome III - European Development Fund No 6) known as SOPACMAPS, and was awarded to IFREMER.
