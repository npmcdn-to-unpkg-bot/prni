  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… The Pacific Islands Applied Geoscience Commission, SOPAC, carried out a marine survey for Papua New Guinea around, Madang, Sissano and Vanimo. ¶ This report describes the high-resolution bathymetric mapping survey carried out over a period ¶ of three weeks from 28 May to 15 June 2006, resulting in the acquisition of over 733 line-km of ¶ MBES data. The survey achieved variable coverage of 50–100% (less for shallow water areas) ¶ of the seafloor from approximately 50 m in the inshore area to an average offshore distance of 4 ¶ km, reaching maximum water depths of some 2000 m. ¶ The dashed line in Bathymetric map of Papua New Guinea illustrates approximate boundary of PNG Exclusive Economic Zone (EEZ). The wave and current regime inside Madang lagoon and Vanimo harbour was also investigated. ¶ This work was initiated by the SOPAC/EU Reducing Vulnerability of Pacific ACP States Project
