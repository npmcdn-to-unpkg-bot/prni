  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… Bathymetric maps of the four harbours in the Cook Islands, Avatiu ,Avarua, and Avana harbour in Raratonga and Arutanga in Aitutaki. Project was completed using an Echotrac single beam 200-kHz sounder with DGPS for navigation control based on UTM Zone 4 WGS84 Spheroid. For tide corrections, data retrieved from the gauge sited in Avatiu Harbour was used to reduce all soundings for the harbours surveyed on Rarotonga. All data has been reduced to tide gauge zero with is reported to be 0.02 m above Lowest Astronomical Tide 92-93 as established by the National Tidal Facility Project. For the Arutanga Harbour, Aitutaki, tidal reduction of soundings was based on measured data from a triton water level gauge. ¶  ¶ Jetprobing of the sediments in Avatiu Harbour show the harbour floor to be very coarse gravel to cobbles. Holes toward the harbour centre had a higher content of sand.
