  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… SOPAC conducted a multibeam survey of the entire lagoon of Penrhyn Atoll, Cook Islands, on behalf of the Marine Resources Department. A three weeks mapping project primarily funded through New Zealand’s bilateral assistance to the Cook Islands, and its ongoing support of strengthening the multibeam mapping capacity of SOPAC . A 200m east to west line spacing along the entire length of the lagoon was surveyed, completely covering the entire lagoon. The resultant datasets are available for download here and related reports are available separately as a different metadata entry.
