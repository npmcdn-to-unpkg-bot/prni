  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………… This record holds the underlying bathymetric grid files of Noro Island of the Western Province in the Solomon Islands . High-resolution bathymetric mapping survey was conducted for the seabed surrounding the island of Noro. The survey was carried out from the 12 to the 15 July 2005 using Reason 8160 MBES equipment and from the 09th July to 11 July 2005, 13-14 July 2005 and from the 18-19 July 2005 using Reason 8101 MBES ,resulting in the acquisition of part of over a total of 1 535 km of multibeam echosounder (MBES) data collected for the Solomon Islands. The resultant data was also used to produce bathymetry charts of Noro at 1:25 000, available from a separate record. This work was initiated by the SOPAC/EU Reducing Vulnerability of Pacific ACP States Project (Task no.: NR 1.1.1). Bathymetry datasets is available as XYZ text files and can be downloaded from the appropriate link under Distribution Info with appropriate permission from the Secretariat.
