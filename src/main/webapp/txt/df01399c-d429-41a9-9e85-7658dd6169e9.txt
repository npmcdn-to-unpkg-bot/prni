  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………… This record holds the underlying bathymetric grid files of the seabed in the nearshore areas of Honiara, in the Solomon Islands. The survey was carried from the 15th of June using the Reason 8160 MBES and from the 20-26/07/2005 using a Reason 8101 MBES, resulting in the acquisition of part of the total , over 1 535 km of multibeam echosounder (MBES) data collected for the Solomon Islands survey. The resultant data was also used to produce bathymetry charts of Gizo at 1:25 000, available from a separate record. This work was initiated by the SOPAC/EU Reducing Vulnerability of Pacific ACP States Project (Task no.: NR 1.1.1). Bathymetry datasets is available as XYZ text files and can be downloaded from the appropriate link under Distribution Info with appropriate permission from the Secretariat.
