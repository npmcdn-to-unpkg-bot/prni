  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This metadata entry holds a gridded bathymetry data of Momi Bay, Fiji Islands, 1996. Original dataset are stored in Universal Traverse Mercator (UTM) WGS84 which are referenced to MapInfo standard projection. Datasets are available for download from the Distribution section of this entry as MapInfo files, ASCII tab delimited files and as Shapefiles, with appropriate permission from the Secretariat. The survey report is also available for download. The contour dataset of the area in .dxf file and in ESRII shapefiles. Survey was conducted by the South Pacific Applied Geoscience Commission (SOPAC) and the Mineral Resources Department, Fiji (MRD) between 5 and 20 December, 1996, to fulfill the requirements of Task FJ 9603. Bathymetry data was gathered as part of the preliminary results of a field survey to assess the potential of detrital gold in areas in and adjacent to Momi Bay west Viti Levu, Fiji. Approximately 45 line ¶ kilometres of data was acquired. This survey was funded principally by the Government of the Republic of Fiji with additional support from the Government of Australia and the ¶ European Union.
