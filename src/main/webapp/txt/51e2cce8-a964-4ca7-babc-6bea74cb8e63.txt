  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… SOPAC conducted a reconnaissance geophysical and geological sampling survey for insular phosphate at Nukulaelae and Nukufetau atolls, Tuvalu, during April and May, 1989. ¶ At Nukulaelae 80 km of bathymetry and 3.5 kHz sub-bottom profiling was carried out, together with onshore and lagoon ¶ sampling and sample analysis from rock samples taken from the reef flats and the islands. An additional 60 km of profiling was completed in two areas of interest at Nukufetau. ¶ These datasets of the two islands are available in GIS formats. ¶  ¶ Beach profiles were established on the islands of Fangaua and Savave at Nukulaelae and Nukufetau respectively. ¶ Exploratory dives were carried out at Nukulaelae and Nukufetau to assess black coral potential. ¶  ¶ A project conducted with the support of the government of Tuvalu and its people and the Commonwealth Fund for Technical Co-operation, the US Geological Survey and ESCAP/UNDP contributed to the survey.
