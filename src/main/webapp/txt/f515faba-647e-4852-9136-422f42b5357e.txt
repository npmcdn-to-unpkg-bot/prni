  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………… This record holds the underlying bathymetric grid files of Marovo Island of the Western Province in the Solomon Islands . High-resolution bathymetric mapping survey was conducted for the seabed surrounding the island of Marovo. The survey was carried out from the 1st of July 2005 to the 6th of July 2005 using Reason 8160 MBES equipment and from the 30th of June to the 7th of July 2005 using Reason 8101 MBES ,resulting in the partially acquiring of over a total of 1 535 km of multibeam echosounder (MBES) data collected for the Solomon Islands.  The resultant data was also used to produce bathymetry charts of Gizo at 1:25 000, available from a separate record. This work was initiated by the SOPAC/EU Reducing Vulnerability of Pacific ACP States Project (Task no.: NR 1.1.1). Bathymetry datasets is available as XYZ text files and can be downloaded from the appropriate link under Distribution Infor with appropriate permission from the Secretariat.
