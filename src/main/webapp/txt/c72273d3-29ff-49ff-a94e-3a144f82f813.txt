  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This record holds the topography of Guam Island (Sheet B) Umatac region with contour interval of 20 feet and dashed lines representing 10 foot contours. The map is part of the Guam Project (Sheets A -  Sheets K) mapped by the Pacific Area, Geological Survey of the United States Department of the Interior Geological Survey that was conducted in 1968. Datum is the mean lower low sea water and depth curves and soundings in Feet. This is an unedited copy of the original manuscript including field additions made in 1968. Map can be downloaded from the distribution Info section of this record with appropriate permission from the Secretariat.
