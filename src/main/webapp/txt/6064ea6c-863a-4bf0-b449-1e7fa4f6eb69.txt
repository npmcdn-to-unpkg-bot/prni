  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This record holds the gridded bathymetry dataset from a dredge site in Fongafale, Tuvalu in 1993. ¶ A De 719e Raytheon precision echo sounder was used for profiling the seabed. Sixty lines of bathymetry profile data were collected. This bathymetry dataset is the result from a  bathymetric survey of the pilot dredge area following dredging, carried out to fulfill the requirements of SOPAC Task 93.TU11. Results of the complete survey is collated into a final report . Links to the related reports and the bathymetry datasets can be accessed from the 'Distribution Info Section' of this metadata record with appropriate permission from the Secretariat.
