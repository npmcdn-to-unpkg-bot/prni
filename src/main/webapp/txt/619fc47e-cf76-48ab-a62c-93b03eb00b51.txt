  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This record holds a series of bathymetric maps of the Northern North Fiji Basin in the Fiji Islands. Map was produced from the scientific cruise SO-35 (Sonne Cruise) conducted between December 6th 1984 to February 6th 1985. The cruise investigated 2 areas in the Lau Basin and 2 in the North Fiji Basin. The contour intervals ranges from 1700m to 3800m. ¶ Attached in this record are 2 different representations of bathymetric maps of the Northern North Fiji Basin. ¶  ¶  ¶ A copy of the map can be downloaded by selecting the appropriate link under 'Online resources' with permission from the Secretariat.

Oxide and sulfide hydrothermal mineralization is ¶ found associated with the young volcanics of ¶ both basins. (Lau basin and North Fiji Basin)The North Fiji Basin is located in the convergence zone between the Pacific and Austral- India Plates. It lies between 2 subduction zones with opposite and convergent polarities, the Tonga zone to the east and the Vanuatu zone to the west.These 2 subduction zones are linked to each other by fault zones one of which the Hunter Fracture Zone constitutes the southern boundary of the basin.
