  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This record holds the wave energy time series datasets ,collected from two positions: Position 1-shallow area, Position 2 - a more exposed and deep water area in the offshore areas of south Tongatapu, Tonga, beginning in 1987. Position 1 measurement began in May 1987 and second position measurements started in December 1989. Data was collected using a Waveriderbuoy. Instrumentation system consists of 3 main component, a) Datawell Waveriderbuoy ,b) Data processing in the Waveriderbuoy, c) Satellite transmission the Argos system ¶  ¶ Datasets are held in .txt file format and is accompanied with an excel sheet and survey reports. ¶  ¶ The wave power data collection programme in the South Pacific started in 1987 to 1995. The work was initially financed by the Norwegian Agency for Development Cooperation, NORAD and organized through an agreement between the Norwegian Engineering Council on Oceanic Resources, NECOR, ¶ and the Norwegian Hydrotechnical Laboratory, NHL a member of the SINTEF Group. In 1990, the Oceanographic Company of Norway A/S, OCEANOR, signed an agreement with NECOR and took over NHL's role in the project. The wave measurement programme provided technical support to the intergovernmental organization SOPAC (South Pacific Applied Geoscience Commission) through its Technical Secretariat (TechSec) in Suva, Fiji. ¶  ¶  ¶  ¶ These datasets are held at the SOPAC Secretariat and can be downloaded from the appropriate link under Distribution Info with appropriate permission from the Secretariat.
