  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This record holds the GIS layers for the deepsea mineral survey conducted within the EEZ of Marshall Islands in 1996, 1998 and 2002 for the cooperative Study on the Deepsea Mineral Resources in Selected Offshore Areas of the SOPAC Region Project. Nine and four seamounts (MS01 - 13) within the Republic of the Marshall Islands were surveyed. The survey composed mainly of MBES topographic survey for preparing detailed topographic maps of the seamounts, and of sampling by chain bag dredge, arm dredge and unconsolidated sediments by SBP, and microtopography survey by SSS. Datasets in shapefiles and MapInfo format can be downloaded from the appropriate link under Distribution Infor of this metadata record with appropriate permission from the Secretariat. Thumbnail shows locations of seamount surveyed.
  │   ├─Purpose………………………………………………………………………………………… The purpose of the survey lies in the assessment of the potential of cobalt rich manganese crust (cobalt crust) resources and the environmental characteristic within the Exclusive Economic Zone (EEZ) of the Republic of the Marshall Islands, a member country of SOPAC.
