  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… A Joint Basic Study by the Applied Geoscience and Technology Division (SOPAC) and the Government of Japan undertaken for marine geological and other studies relating to mineral prospecting to assess the mineral resources potential of the deep sea bottom in the offshore regions of SOPAC member countries. This metadata entry presents survey carried out in the Exclusive Economic Zone of the Republic of Kiribati., for the Fiscal Year 1991. Bathymetry dataset was produced by the Joint Japan International Cooperation Agency and SOPAC Deepsea Mineral Survey (MFES survey of Manganese Nodules). Survey was conducted from August 24, 1991 to February 8, 1992 . Work included Positioning ,topographic and acoustic surveys, magnetic survey (PGM Survey), sea floor observation by CDC and photography, sampling and CTD measurements. Bathymetry datasets can be downloaded from the appropriate link under Distribution Info with appropriate permission from the Secretariat.This bathymetry dataset was produced by the Joint Japan International Cooperatiopn Agency and SOPAC Deepsea Mineral Survey of selected areas within the SOPAC region. The survey comprises ship survey (acoustic sounding survey and sampling survey) and the data analysis for assessment of mineral resources and an environmental survey as a baseline study to understand the environmental situation, for future evaluation of potential mining impacts on the deep sea environment. The cobalt crust survey in the Kiribati EEZ: has covered sea mounts and other features in the selected areas.
