  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… A project conducted by SOPAC using a high-resolution swath mapping, using multibeam echosounder to delineate the seabed morphology of Apia harbor, Vaiusu bay and the fore-reef area fronting Apia. Computer processing of swath mapping, produced data visualizations that render complex three-dimensional concepts into simple, informative,color. ¶ From the resultant data collected ,bathymetry maps of the dredge pit blue holes,modeled marina access channel, and Apia Harbor was created and is available for download from its appropriate link under distribution info of this metadata record. ¶ The field survey for this study was carried out during the month of February 2002, with the Multibeam survey completed from the 15th to the 28th of February.
