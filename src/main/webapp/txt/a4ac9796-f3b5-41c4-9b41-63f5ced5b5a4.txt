  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This record holds a bathymetric profile chart of the Central basin of Vanuatu collected by the R/V S.P Lee cruise L6-82-SP ( Leg 2)in the South Pacific Ocean. The chart was prepared by T.E Chase, B.A Seekins, and M.A Cloud in 1982. Leg 2 was conducted in the Central Basin of Vanuatu, included 2,520 km of 24 fold, CDP multi-channel seismic –reflection profiles. In addition , 622 km of high resolution Uniboom  seismic reflection profiles and nearly 4,500 km of 3.5 kHz very high resolution seismic reflection, 12 kHz bathymetric, magnetic and gravity profiles were collected  and 33 sonobuoy wide angle reflection refraction  profiles were obtained. Geophysical trackline spacing was generally 5- 10km apart and in some localities spacing was as close as 1km apart. This chart is part of a series of other charts produced from the 1982 S.P. Lee cruise. ¶  ¶  ¶  ¶  ¶ A copy of the map can be downloaded by selecting the appropriate link under 'Online resources' with permission from the Secretariat.
