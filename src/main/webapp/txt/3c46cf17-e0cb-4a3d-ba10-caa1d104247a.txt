  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This record holds a high resolution bathymetry data that was collected for Abaiang Atoll , Kiribati in 1999. ¶ The 800 kilometres of high resolution bathymetry data collected is the result of the SOPAC assistance to the Kiribati, Ministry of Natural Resources Development to develop marine resources management plans for Abaiang Atoll. ¶ Five sites were occupied to measure the flow. Three current meters were deployed in five locations during the survey period. Two water level recorders were also deployed to monitor tide .Twenty seven stations in the lagoon were monitored for salinity ,temperature and dissolved oxygen, measured with and turbidity determined with the aid of a secchi disk. Bottom sediments were also taken at each of these locations. For longer term monitoring , six long term temperature loggers were deployed for a period of up to twelve months. ¶ Dataset developed was used to create a hydrodynamic model of the lagoon circulation that models a number of water quality parameters. Data was also used to assist with a survey of coral species , abundance and distribution implemented by the Kiribati Ministry in 1999. ¶ Bathymetry datasets can be downloaded from the appropriate link under Distribution Info with appropriate permission from the Secretariat.
