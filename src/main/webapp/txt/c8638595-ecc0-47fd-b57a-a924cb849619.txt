  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This bathymetry dataset was produced by the Joint Japan International Cooperation Agency and SOPAC Deepsea Mineral Survey of selected areas within the SOPAC region. The survey comprises ship survey (acoustic sounding survey and sampling survey) and the data analysis for assessment of mineral resources and an environmental survey as a baseline study to understand the environmental situation, for future evaluation of potential mining impacts on the deep sea environment. The cobalt crust survey in the Tonga EEZ: has covered sea mounts and other features in the selected areas.
