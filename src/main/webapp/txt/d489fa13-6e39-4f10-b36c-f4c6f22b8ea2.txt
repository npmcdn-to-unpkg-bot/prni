This metadata entry presents survey conducted in the sea area of the Republic of the Marshall Islands, for the Year 1988. Bathymetry dataset was produced by the Joint Japan International Cooperatiopn Agency and SOPAC Deepsea Mineral Survey.  Survey included Position locating using GPS, acoustic surveys seafloor (topographic survey) , sea floor observation and photography, core sampling and CTD measurements.
