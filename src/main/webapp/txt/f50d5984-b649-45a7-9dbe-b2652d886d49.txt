  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… A SOPAC multibeam bathymetric survey and a single channel seismic survey for the Lautoka Port area, Fiji Islands. ¶ High-resolution swath mapping, using multibeam echosounders, was conducted to survey close to 100 per cent of the sea floor and for the seismic data, an interpretation was completed for 4 lines that parallel the north, east and ¶ west faces of the wharf, and a fifth line that parallels the Lautoka foreshore east of the Queens Wharf to delineate bedrock in the subsurface. ¶ The survey was completed with the support of the Mineral Resources Department and the ¶ Maritime and Ports Authority of Fiji. Both are gratefully acknowledge. Tidal information provided ¶ by the National Tidal Facility, Flinders University, South Australia is gratefully acknowledge. Resultant dataset for the survey is presented here while the related report is presented in a separate metadata entry. ¶ This survey is the second in a series of surveys to carry out multibeam and single channel ¶ seismic mapping of the major ports of entry into Fiji.
