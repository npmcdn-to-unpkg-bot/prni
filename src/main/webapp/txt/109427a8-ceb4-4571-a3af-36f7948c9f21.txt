  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… SOPAC conducted bathymetry survey and jet probing in Vaiusu Bay. ¶ An Odom echotrac precision echo sounder was used for profiling the seabed and 60 jetprobe holes were drilled into the lagoon floor in the area surveyed to confirm sediment thickness, obtain samples for grain size analysis, and determine sediment distribution and composition from the jetprobe locations. Depth of penetration data combined with the bathymetry data an isopach, a map of sediment thickness was generated to calculate the resource volume. ¶ Tab delimited point XYZ files of location of Jet Probes are available for download from its appropriate link under Distribution Info. ¶  ¶ The survey WS9501 carried out to fulfill the requirements of Task 95.WS11 . This survey was carried out at the request of W.S.
