  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This record holds the Acoustic Imagery Map for Iron Bottom Sound, Central Solomon Trough (CST) in the Solomon Islands. This map is a working copy and is part of a new sheet assemblage adopted for the final results that was produced from reprocessing of the data by IFREMER during the SOPACMAPS Project Leg 2. The SOPACMAPS Project Leg 2 was funded by the European Community and conducted by the oceanographical research vessel L'Atalante from 19 July 1993 to 20 October 1993. The map shows a backscatter imagery of the Iron Bottom Sound during SOPACMAPS Leg 2, between Guadalcanal, Florida and the Savo Islands. A copy of the map can also be downloaded by selecting the appropriate link under 'Online resources' with permission from the Secretariat.
