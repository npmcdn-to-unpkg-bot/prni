  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… SOPAC conducted a high-resolution swath mapping, using multibeam echosounders to delineate the seabed morphology ¶ in the vicinity of the wreck USS Mississinewa located in water depths of around 40m in Ulithi Atoll. A second area was mapped in Ulithi Atoll along the NE eastern ¶ margin of the lagoon from Masu to Asor island. Results of this mapping did not delineate any extraordinary feature other than the existing morphology of the seabed. The most interesting feature seen where elliptical shaped mounds which may imply bi-direction bottom currents of ¶ similar magnitudes. The mapping of Yap harbour was also completed. These results have been presented in map ¶ form as contours and as soundings. The resultant datasets are available here and the related reports is available from a different metadata entry here on the SOPAC Geonetwork site.
