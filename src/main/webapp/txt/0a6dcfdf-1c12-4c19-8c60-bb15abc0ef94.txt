  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… The Pacific Islands Applied Geoscience Commission, SOPAC, carried out a marine survey in the waters around Nauru. ¶ High-resolution bathymetric mapping survey of the seabed surrounding the island of Nauru. The survey achieved 100 % coverage of the sea floor from approximately 50 m depth in the nearshore area to an average offshore distance of 3 km, reaching water depths of some 1800 m. ¶ A review of external sources of available bathymetry was also undertaken, and the survey data was supplemented with publicly available data for Nauru EEZ. The resultant data ¶ compilation was used to produce a bathymetry chart of Nauru at 1:25 000. The resultant datasets in ascii, tab delimiter files are available from this metadata entry. The charts and related reports are available as separate metadata entries here on the SOPAC Geonetwork site. ¶ This work was initiated by the SOPAC/EU Reducing Vulnerability of Pacific ACP States ¶ Project.
