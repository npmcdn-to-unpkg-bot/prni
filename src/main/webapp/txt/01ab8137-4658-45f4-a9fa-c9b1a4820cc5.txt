  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… A project conducted by SOPAC using a high-resolution swath mapping, using multibeam echosounder to delineate the seabed morphology of Apia harbor, Vaiusu bay and the fore-reef area fronting Apia. Mechanical vector average RCM Aanderaa meter with a vane and paddle wheel units 7285  and 7286 and a Sontek Triton Acoustic Doppler Profiler was used to measure currents at three locations in the study area. ¶ Data recorded are also contained as tabular data in PDF format. ¶ Bathymetric maps of the three areas are in delimited file formats. Computer processing of swath mapping, produced data visualizations that render complex three-dimensional concepts into simple, informative, color. Current were measured at three locations in the study area. ¶ The field survey for this study was carried out during the month of February 2002, with the Multibeam survey completed from the 15th to the 28th of February.
