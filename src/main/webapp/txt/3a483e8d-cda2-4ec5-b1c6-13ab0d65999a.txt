  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This record shows the bathymetry map of Rennell Islands area in the Solomon Island. The map is past of the  USGS Cruise L7-82-SP in the Solomon Islands region, 1982. Data were collected by the the U.S. Geological Survey (USGS) Research Vessel S. P. LEE during Cruise L782SP to study the bathymetric, seismic and other related data. The study showed the presence of 'a small basin containing up to 1 1/2 seconds of sediments between Rennell island and Indispensable Reef. The depth of water over the basin, exceeding 600 metres, the minimal sediment thickness and the small extent of the basin, do not make it a prime target for investigation at this time. However, since the geophysical data available in the area was not geared for hydrocarbon resource investigation, but rather for oceanic reconnaissance. A copy of the map can be downloaded by selecting the appropriate link under 'Online resources' with permission from the Secretariat.
