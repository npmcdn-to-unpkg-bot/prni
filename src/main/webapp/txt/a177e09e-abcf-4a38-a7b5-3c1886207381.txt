  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This record holds the XYZ ASCII Tab delimited text file format of the bathymetry of Forari Bay, East Efate Island in Vanuatu. The dataset is the result of survey conducted by SOPAC for the investigation of nearshore seabed around the island of Efate using a multibeam echosounder (MBES). The survey was carried out over a period of 25 days in August 2003, resulting in the acquisition of over 600 line kilometres of MBES data. Coverage of the seafloor included the inshore area, starting from a minimum depth of 40 m, to an average offshore distance of 10 km, reaching water depths of some 2 000 m. A 15 by 15 km area over a 100 to 150 m plateau, situated approximately 80 km to the SSW of Mele Bay, was also surveyed.  Dataset should not be used for navigation purposes. A 1:20 000 bathymetry chart of the dataset is available as a separate metadata record. This work was initiated by the SOPAC/EU Reducing Vulnerability of Pacific ACP States Project .
