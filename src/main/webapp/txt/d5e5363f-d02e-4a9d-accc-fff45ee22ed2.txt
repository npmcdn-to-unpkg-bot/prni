  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This record holds the bathymetry datasets from the high resolution bathymetric mapping conducted in the 9 atolls of Tuvalu. The Pacific Islands Applied Geoscience Commission (SOPAC) carried out a marine survey for Tuvalu in the waters around all nine atolls and low reef islands, namely, Nanumea, ¶ Niutao, Nanumanga, Nui, Vaitupu, Nukufetau, Funafuti, Nukulaelae, Niulakita. Conducted for a period of 5 weeks in September and October 2004.. The survey achieved good coverage of the seafloor from approximately 10m depth in the nearshore reef slope area, to an average offshore depth of some 2000m, at an average slope angle of 27°. The resultant data was used to produce nine bathymetry charts for Tuvalu at scales ranging from 1:20 000 to 1:50 000. The Charts and related reports are available from separate metadata records. This work was initiated by the SOPAC/EU Reducing Vulnerability of Pacific ACP States Project, under the European Development Fund (EDF9).
