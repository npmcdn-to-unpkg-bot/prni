  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This record holds 4 high resolution bathymetry datasets of Banaba near shore areas, in Kiribati. The datasets are in XYZ tab delimited format . This includes a 10m grid dataset of all points, a separate 10m, 20m and a 50m grid dataset. The survey was carried in October 2005, resulting in the acquisition of over 900 km of multibeam echo sounder (MBES) data. Data compilation was also used to produce bathymetry charts and is available for download as a different record. Datasets are available for download from this record under Distribution Info. This work was initiated by the Pacific Islands Applied Geoscience Commission (SOPAC) and the European Union (EU) Reducing Vulnerability of Pacific ACP States Project.
