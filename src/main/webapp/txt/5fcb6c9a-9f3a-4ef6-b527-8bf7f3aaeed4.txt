  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This record holds the bathymetry data of Apolima Strait, Samoa in 10 m grid. The Apolima Strait separates the two largest islands of Samoa, the island of Savai'i to the northwest, and Upolu to the southeast. Data contained here is part of the resultant dataset from the SPC-SOPAC division marine survey carried for the islands of Savai'i and Upolu. High-resolution bathymetric mapping survey, resulting in the acquisition of over 2754 line-km of MBES data for the whole of Samoa Survey. The entire survey achieved 100% coverage of the seafloor from approximately 50 m depth in the nearshore area to an average offshore distance of 10 km, reaching water depths of some 1500 m. Data is in ascii tab delimited format and is available from the Distribution Info of this record with appropriate permission from the secretariat.This work was initiated by the SOPAC/EU Reducing Vulnerability of Pacific ACP States Project, under the European Development Fund.
