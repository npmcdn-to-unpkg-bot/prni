  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… SOPAC conducted a high-resolution swath mapping, using multibeam echosounders to delineate the seabed morphology ¶ in the vicinity of the wreck USS Mississinewa located in water depths of around 40m in Ulithi Atoll. A second area was mapped in Ulithi Atoll along the NE eastern ¶ margin of the lagoon from Masu to Asor island. Results of this mapping did not delineate any extraordinary feature other than the existing morphology of the seabed. The most interesting feature seen where elliptical shaped mounds which may imply bi-direction bottom currents of ¶ similar magnitudes. The mapping of Yap harbor was also completed. These results have been presented in map ¶ form as contours and as soundings. The related Report for the survey is downloadable from here and the resultant datasets of the survey is available as a separate metadata entry here on the SOPAC geonetwork site.
