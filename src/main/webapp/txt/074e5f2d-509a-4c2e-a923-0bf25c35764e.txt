(1500m/s) Map is drawn on a 100 metre contour interval. Map also has an insert of data sources showing seabeam surveys, seabeam tracks and single beam. Digital soundings are supplied by the National Geophysical Data Center, USA. Bathymetric plotting sheet 354, supplied by the RNZN Hydrographic Office.
