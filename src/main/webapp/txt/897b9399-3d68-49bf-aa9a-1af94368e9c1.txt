  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This record holds a diagram of a Seismic Profiles Interpretation Map for the East of Malaita, Solomon Islands. The Seismic Reflection Profiles is part of the results, collected from the SOPACMAPS Project Leg 2 that was conducted by the oceanographical research vessel L'Atalante in August 28-29 of 1993. The survey included the mapping of bathymetry, investigating of resource potential and the studying of geological processes, including geological hazards. Refer to report under the 'Online Resource' section for the interpretation of the seismic reflection profiles map. A copy of the map can also be downloaded by selecting the appropriate link under 'Online resources' with permission from the Secretariat.
