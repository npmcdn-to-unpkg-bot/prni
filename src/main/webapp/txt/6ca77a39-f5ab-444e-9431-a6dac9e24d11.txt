  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This bathymetry dataset was produce by the Joint Japan International Cooperatiopn Agency and SOPAC Deepsea Mineral Survey of selected areas within the SOPAC region. The survey comprises ship survey (acoustic sounding survey and sampling survey) and the data analysis for assessment of mineral resources and an enviromental survey as a baseline study to understand the enviromental situation, for future evaluation of potential mining impacts on the deep sea enviroment. The cobalt crust survey in the Marshall Islands EEZ: has covered thirteen sea mounts and other features in the selected surveyed areas.
