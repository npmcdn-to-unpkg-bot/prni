  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This entry holds a gridded bathymetry data of the North West and South West of the Yasawa Islands, Fiji Islands collected in 1996. Original dataset are stored in Universal Traverse Mercator (UTM) WGS84 which are referenced to MapInfo standard projection. Bathymetry datasets are available for download from the Distribution section of this entry as MapInfo formats, ,TXT ASCII files and as Shapefiles, with appropriate permission from the Secretariat. Survey was conducted by the South Pacific Applied Geoscience Commission (SOPAC) as part of Task 91.FJ.19e: Bedrock Miocene structure around Yasawa Islands and the top end of Nacula Island in the Yasawas. Funding for the survey was provided by Government of New Zealand.
