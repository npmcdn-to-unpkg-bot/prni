  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This record holds a bathymetric map of a possible OTEC site south of Viti Levu, Fiji Islands. Map was produced from a multibeam echo-sounding survey by the Research Vessel Jean Charcot. The work was coordinated between IFREMER, Mineral Resources Department and CCOP/SOPAC.The map is drawn on a contour interval of 10m and the sound velocity was at 1500m/s. The map was prepared by JP Foucher and JP Maze( IFREMER), navigation under the direction of Robert Smith (MRD) and trisponder supplied by SOPAC. ¶  ¶  ¶ A copy of the map can be downloaded by selecting the appropriate link under 'Online resources' with permission from the Secretariat.
