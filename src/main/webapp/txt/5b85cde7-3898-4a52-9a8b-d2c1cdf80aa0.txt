  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This record hold the bathymetry map of the Yasawa group in Western side of the Fiji Islands. This was a SOPAC survey of the Yasawa Islands ,Fiji using single beam and multibeam bathymetry surveys to delineate the seabed morphology of Yasawa Islands of Waya and Naviti, mapping an area for a potential new deepwater passage leading into Lautoka. The Bathymetry map in PDF format is available from the distribution section of this record for download with permission from the secretariat.
