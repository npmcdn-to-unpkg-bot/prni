Suva Harbour and Laucala Bay are two large lagoons separated in part by Suva Peninsula. ¶ Combined, these two lagoons cover an area of around 74 km2. The lagoons are well protected by ¶ an extensive barrier reef complex breached by three navigable channels one in Suva harbour and ¶ two in Laucala Bay. A channel that traverses the perimeter of Suva Peninsula links Suva Harbour ¶ and Laucala Bay.
