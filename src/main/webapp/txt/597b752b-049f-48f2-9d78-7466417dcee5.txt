  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This record holds the underlying bathymetric grid files of seabed around the island of Tongatapu in Tonga. Datasets are available in 10m grid, 30m grid and 5m grid. Fieldwork was undertaken over a period of 5 weeks from 13 November 2003 to 7 December 2003 by the Applied Geoscience and Technology Division (SOPAC) of SPC. Survey conducted in Tongatapu achieved part of the 100% coverage of the seafloor from approximately 50 to 100 m depth nearshore area to an average offshore distance of 8 km, reaching maximum water depths of some 1200 m for the Tongan survey. ¶ Bathymetry datasets is available as XYZ text files and can be downloaded from the appropriate link under Distribution Info with appropriate permission from the Secretariat. This work was initiated by the SOPAC/EU project ‘Reducing ¶ Vulnerability of Pacific ACP States.
