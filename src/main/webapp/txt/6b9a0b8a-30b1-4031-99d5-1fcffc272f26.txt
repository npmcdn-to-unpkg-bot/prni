  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… SOPAC conducted a multibeam bathymetric survey and a single channel seismic survey for the Lautoka Port area, Fiji islands. High-resolution swath mapping, using multibeam echosounders, was conducted to survey close to 100 per cent of the sea floor and for the seismic data, an interpretation was completed for 4 lines that parallel the north, east and ¶ west faces of the wharf, and a fifth line that parallels the Lautoka foreshore east of the Queens Wharf to delineate bedrock in the subsurface. This survey is the second in a series of surveys to carry out multibeam and single channel seismic mapping of the major ports of entry into Fiji. Resulting dataset of the survey is presented as a separate metadata entry while the report is available for download from this metadata entry.
