  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This record holds a bathymetric map (seabeam) of the Northern Lau Basin in the Fiji Islands. Map was produced from the scientific cruise SO-48 (Sonne Cruise). The contour intervals on the map ranges from 1100m to 3500m. The cruise was focused in carrying out detailed geoscientific investigations and was conducted in 3 legs (hydrothermalism leg 1, 2 and 3) from February 3,1987 to March 3, 1987. A copy of the map can be downloaded by selecting the appropriate link under 'Online resources' with permission from the Secretariat.
