  │   ├─Abstract……………………………………………………………………………………… This record holds velocity profiles (CDP 1003) of Solomon Islands Area, Line 37. The 2D Re-processing Project was awarded to Digital Exploration Limited by SOPAC and funded by the European Development Fund. Line 37 in the Solomon Islands was processed using tapes which were from data already stacked by previous processing contractors and also from the original field data tapes. Map was produced from Original pre-stack processing by U.S.Geological Survey, R/V S.P.Lee, L7-82-SP, May 1982 and Original pre-stack processing by U.S. Geological Survey, R/V S.P.Lee, L6-84-SP, 1984/1985 and Shell 1971 pre-stack processing, Shell Internationale, M.V.Petrel, Shell 71, May 1971 and Mobile 1972 pre-stack processing, Mobile Exploration Services, S-7, Mobil - 72, June 1972. The objective of the processing were to obtain good definition of any reef structures present in the area, good resolution of the basement sections and to eliminate any long period, high amplitude, water bottom multiples. A copy of the map can be downloaded by selecting the appropriate link under 'Online resources' with permission from the Secretariat.
